# Citation Policy

Every factual page should prefer official or verifiable references. A reference may be a publisher announcement, developer blog, store page, standards document, or reputable editorial source.

| Claim type | Recommended source |
|---|---|
| Release date | Official announcement or store page. |
| Platform support | Store page or publisher documentation. |
| Developer and publisher | Official website or press kit. |
| Update history | Patch notes or changelog. |
